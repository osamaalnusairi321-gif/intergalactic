How it works

Nature already moves electrons. We design around it.

01 — Feed

Organic material enters the cell.

Food waste and other biodegradable material provide the substrate that supports microbial activity.

02 — Convert

Microbes release electrons.

As microorganisms break down organic compounds, electrons can be captured by an electrode system.

03 — Power

The electron flow becomes current.

The system routes captured electrons through a circuit, producing measurable electrical output.
